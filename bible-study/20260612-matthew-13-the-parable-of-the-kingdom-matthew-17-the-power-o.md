## 💡 Main Themes

* Humble Beginnings to Massive Growth
* The Kingdom of God started tiny (with Jesus and a few disciples).
   * It grows dynamically into a massive, global spiritual community. [7, 8, 9, 10, 11] 
* Quality Over Quantity in Faith
* You do not need a large amount of faith to see breakthrough.
   * Even the smallest amount of genuine, living faith can unleash divine power. [12] 
* Shelter and Inclusion
* The grown mustard plant provides a place for "birds of the air" to nest.
   * This symbolizes the Church becoming a place of safety and welcome for all nations. [13, 14] 
* Hidden Potential
* Unseen spiritual life packs immense power.
   * God consistently uses the small, weak, and overlooked things of the world to achieve great purposes. [15, 16]
